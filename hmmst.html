The Poems of HORIZ|MAG|STORM|SHIFT|TRANS

The HORIZ|MAG|STORM|SHIFT|TRANS event at the Galateea Gallery in January-February 2014, was projected in 5 acts. Each act has a textual predetermination enclosed in the spaceform of a poem statement. The verses were read on, recorded and composed over a soundtrack made for the event.
