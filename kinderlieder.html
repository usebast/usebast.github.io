2015 Kinderlieder Youtube Channel Animations

2D Animation for the YouTube Kinderlieder Channel, specialized in producing short film based on old German songs for kids.
During the production stages we delivered the whole pipeline, from ideas, storyboards, character designs, animations and video editing and compositing with the soundtracks provided by the client.
